Before you commence the operation it is necessary to ascertain that the power has been completely extinguished from the aircraft system in order to avoid damage during the procedure.

Utilize a screwdriver to remove all the small fasteners from the panel because if you do not ensure that they are all disconnected properly there could be complications during the reassembly process.

It is prohibited to perform this maintenance activity without the prior written authorization from the responsible engineering office which might delay the work and create unnecessary problems for the technician.
